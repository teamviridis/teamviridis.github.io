About The Project

Agriculture consumes 70% of global freshwater. Climate change and drought increase the urgency for smarter solutions. Our Smart IoT Irrigation System uses real-time sensors and automation to reduce water waste and improve plant growth efficiency.

System Architecture

ESP32 Controller

The brain of the system, processing sensor data and controlling irrigation logic.

Environmental Sensors

Soil moisture, rain detection, temperature, humidity, and pH monitoring.

Automated Irrigation

Water pump activates only when soil moisture is below threshold and rain is not detected.

IoT Monitoring

Remote monitoring via WiFi & MQTT dashboard for real-time data access.

Innovation & Impact

Water Efficiency

Prevents over-irrigation and reduces evaporation through underground delivery.

Sustainability

Supports climate-resilient agriculture and smart farming practices.

Low-Cost & Scalable

Designed using accessible components for practical implementation.
