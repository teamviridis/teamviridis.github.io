About The Project

Agriculture consumes 70% of global freshwater. Climate change and drought make efficient water use critical. Our Smart IoT Irrigation System integrates real-time environmental sensing and automation to reduce water waste, improve plant growth, and support sustainable agriculture.

System Architecture

ESP32 Controller

Processes sensor data and controls irrigation logic in real time.

Environmental Sensors

Soil moisture, rainfall detection, temperature, humidity, and pH monitoring.

Automated Irrigation

Water pump activates only when soil is dry and rain is not detected.

IoT Monitoring

WiFi-based remote monitoring via dashboard and data logging.

Innovation & Impact

Water Efficiency

Prevents over-irrigation and reduces evaporation losses.

Sustainability

Supports climate-resilient farming and data-driven agriculture.

Scalable Design

Low-cost, modular architecture suitable for real-world expansion.
